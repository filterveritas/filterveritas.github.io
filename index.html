The name

FILTER stands for filtering, clarifying, purifying, of the information and sensations we get, with our common sense, reasoning and critical thought. F.I.L.T.E.R. is the acronym of our six core values: Focus, Imagination, Leadership, Transparency, Empathy and Result. VERITAS stands for the truth and the true principle or belief.

The team

We are young professionals driven by our responsibility as citizens and people belonging to a community. Our (individual) activism started years earlier. In 2015 we all got together and started acting as an informal group, being part of different projects and initiatives. Throughout the experience, we felt that we can achieve more tangible results and initiate more relevant changes as a legal entity. Therefore, on the 5th of March 2016 we founded a non-governmental not-for-profit organisation. On the 13 of June 2016 we were registered as an association of citizens by the Central Registry of R.Macedonia. Our headquarters are located in Skopje, the capital of the Republic of Macedonia.
